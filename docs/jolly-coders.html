Jolly coders

This page contain material related to the activities of a group of TeXmacs developers.

Jolly coders are a group of people interested in learning, hacking and in general exchanging about TeXmacs internal structure. The group started random gatherings in March 2021 and hope to continue to meet quasi-regularly for long time. For the moment the focus is to learn together about TeXmacs internal structure, the C++ and Scheme codebase, the interaction with external libraries and the various plugins, the behaviour of the converters, and many other topics related to TeXmacs.

Meetings are announced in a private mailing list. Write to m.gubinelli@gmail.com to ask to be included in the communications. Recordings of the meetings are kept whenever possible.

Current members: Basile Audoly, Massimiliano Gubinelli, Joris van der Hoeven, Sebastian Miele, Giovanni Piredda, Joy Yang Qiping, Peter Rapčan, Jeroen Wouters.

Meetings held so far and summary of the topics treated

  • 12.3.2021 – The Xcode project, working on a bug related to exporting PDF images to the clipboard on a Mac.

  • 13.3.2021 – General structure of the C++ codebase, various directories, abstract interfaces to the user interface, handling of events, example of the close window event.

  • 14.3.2021 – Scheme/C++ interface, patching the S7 Scheme interpreter, generation of menus and widgets between C++ and Scheme.
